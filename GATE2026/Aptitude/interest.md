## Interest

### Simple Interest (SI)
- **Formula:**  
    SI = (P × R × T) / 100  
    Where:  
    P = Principal, R = Rate (% per annum), T = Time (years)
- **Trick:**  
    If SI, P, and R are known, T = (SI × 100) / (P × R)

### Compound Interest (CI)
- **Formula:**  
    CI = P × [(1 + R/100)^T – 1]
- **Trick:**  
    For 2 years:  
    CI = SI + (SI × R) / 100

### Quick Tips
- **Difference between CI and SI for 2 years:**  
    Diff = P × (R/100)^2
- **Interest doubles:**  
    For SI: T = 100 / R years
